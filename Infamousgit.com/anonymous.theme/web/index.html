ANONYMOUS

CM11 THEME

Anonymous CM11 Theme is a clean minimal black and grey theme for custom Cyanogen Mod type ROMs.

Like the theme? Support this project and buy the donate version

Custom User interface

The app itself has many awesome features. You can apply the theme right from the app. It has a preview gallery as well as wallpapers that you can apply right from the app.

Requests

You can request apps to be themed from the app and it will be sent to me to work on.

Infamous extra's

Many other great features include a link to my development team and our forum and other sites.

Contact the developer

You get a direct line to the developer (me.) Email any suggestions you have as well as submit wallpapers to be added.

Hide from app drawer

We know not everyone is into having a stand alone user interface for a theme. We added the options to hide the app from the app drawer with one click. The theme still works but no added app.

Rate and Share

Liking the theme? Rate or share the app from a click from within the app. We appreciate all the support!
